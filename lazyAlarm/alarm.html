Help

Create an alarm by specifying a time. Examples of alarms include: 2am, 4PM, 5:10a, 16, 12:20a.

Create a timer by specifying a duration. Examples of timers include: 2h, 12h4s, 60s, 1h1m1s.

You can enter additional text to give your alarm/timer a description. So a full example would be: 3:14p time to eat dunch/linner
